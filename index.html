About Kdell Infotech.

IT recruiting with a practitioner's eye — rooted in hands-on data, cloud, and enterprise experience.

Kdell Infotech is a Georgia-based IT staffing and consulting partner focused on connecting high-performing technology professionals with organizations that need to deliver reliable, secure, and scalable solutions.

From data and analytics to cloud, cybersecurity, QA, and help desk support, we understand the tools and environments our clients use every day. That means we ask better questions, present tighter short-lists, and create smoother experiences for both hiring managers and candidates.

Based in Austell, GA and serving clients nationwide, we work with startups, mid-sized companies, and public sector teams that value clear communication, transparency, and long-term partnerships.
